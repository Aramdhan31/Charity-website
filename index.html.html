Welcome to The Listening Place

We are a non-profit organization dedicated to providing free, confidential, and non-judgmental listening support to individuals facing emotional distress, loneliness, or crisis. We believe that everyone deserves someone to listen without judgment, and we strive to create a safe and supportive space for people to share their stories.
